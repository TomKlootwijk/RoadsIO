Paint Rush.io

Roll, splat, steal territory. Fast rounds for friends.

Desktop: WASD / arrows + Space Mobile: drag joystick + BOOST

Core loop

Every second is readable: your ball leaves paint, enemy paint slowly flips, territory grows your score and body, and bigger bodies are easier to ram.

  • Paint: roll over cells to claim them.
  • Convert: enemy cells resist for a moment, so fights feel tactical.
  • Boost: spend boost to steal, escape, or splat smaller balls.
  • Win: own the most arena after three minutes.
