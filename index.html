Core loop

Every second is readable: your jello ball leaves paint, enemy paint flips under you, territory grows your score and body, and speed plus size decides splats.

  • Paint: roll over cells to claim them.
  • Convert: roll over enemy cells to cover them.
  • Boost: spend boost to steal, escape, or splat smaller balls.
  • Win: own the most arena after ninety seconds.
